# Full Scope Report

## Objective

Create a premium, final-state website concept that shows what the business should feel like to an actual customer while staying grounded in the company's live public facts.

## What To Capture For Any Future Site

1. Real business identity
2. Live contact details
3. Real offers and trust claims
4. Service footprint and geography
5. Real proof assets already available
6. Seasonal or urgency angles
7. Public weaknesses in structure, trust, and conversion

## Build Sequence

1. Audit the live site and extract only verifiable claims.
2. Identify what the business already has that creates trust.
3. Reframe the offer into a stronger user journey.
4. Build a premium visual direction around the business category.
5. Use motion and 3D only where they reinforce hierarchy and trust.
6. Add geography, proof, and call-to-action clarity.
7. Create a sendable PDF and archive trail.
8. Publish to portfolio channels and send for stakeholder review.

## Repeatable Section Model

- Arrival / hero
- Authority strip
- Geography or service footprint
- Service architecture
- Visual proof or gallery
- Seasonal or urgency message
- Offers and incentives
- Family / founder / trust block
- Estimate or contact close

## Quality Standard

- Looks like the finished live site, not a teardown
- Uses only supported claims as facts
- Feels category-specific, not templated
- Works on desktop and mobile
- Has one clear call path
- Includes a portable PDF snapshot

## Reusable Deliverables For Other Clients
