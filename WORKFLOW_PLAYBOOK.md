# Workflow Playbook

## Goal

Turn a weak local-business website into a premium final-state presentation that looks like the finished customer experience, not a teardown.

## Operating Sequence

1. Pull the live site and inner pages.
2. Lock only the facts that are publicly supportable.
3. Extract the trust signals already present.
4. Reframe the offer into a cleaner, higher-conversion structure.
5. Build a category-specific visual direction.
6. Add geography, proof, motion, and call-path clarity.
7. Render voice and score layers.
8. Export screenshots and PDF.
9. Publish to portfolio channels.
10. Archive the full process into Google Drive.

## Build Components

- homepage shell
- typography system
- brand mark
- county and city atlas
- proof gallery
- seasonal urgency block
- savings section
- estimate close
- voice intro
- score bed
- PDF leave-behind

## Reuse Rule

For future sites, keep the structure but swap the business truth, visual language, service geometry, and urgency story to match the category.
